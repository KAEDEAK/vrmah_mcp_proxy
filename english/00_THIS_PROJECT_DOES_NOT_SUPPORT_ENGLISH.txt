This project uses the Japanese speech synthesis engine VOICEVOX.

Therefore, the language used by the agent for speech is limited to Japanese.

For this reason, English is not supported.

Support for English may be possible by changing the speech synthesis engine.

In addition, the lip-sync system for the VRM component is optimized for Japanese vowels and does not support other languages.


To use this in an English environment, the following options are available:

(1) Use a speech synthesis engine other than VOICEVOX.

(2) In the English version of README.md, give up on English at the following section and use Japanese instead.
(For the content of the speech, refer to the Japanese version of README.md.)
Avatar Communication (MCP)

(3) Produce English pronunciation using Japanese phonetics.
There are precedents, but it is said to be difficult.
